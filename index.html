Program Sections

Hockey clubs typically offer a variety of programs for players of all ages and skill levels. These programs can be divided into three main sections:

Junior Program

Youth programs are designed to introduce young players to the game of hockey and teach them the basic skills and knowledge they need to succeed. These programs typically include on-ice instruction, off-ice training, and games.

Teenager Program
